Frequently Asked Questions

Question 1: How do I create an account?

Answer: To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and follow the instructions to provide your personal information and create a username and password.

Question 2: How do I purchase tokens?

Answer: Once you have created an account and logged in, navigate to the "Buy Tokens" section of the website. Select the desired number of tokens and proceed to the payment gateway to complete your purchase.
