How WinWin Works

1

Create an account

Sign up for a free account on WinWin to start buying and selling second-hand items through a raffle system.

2

Buy tokens

Purchase tokens that can be used to buy tickets for different items. Each ticket gives you a chance to win the item at a fraction of its cost.

3

Browse and participate in raffles

Choose from a wide variety of items listed on the platform and use your tokens to buy tickets for the items you're interested in.

4

Sell your items

List your items for sale on the platform, set a desired selling price, and decide how many tickets you want to sell for the raffle. Each ticket will cost one token.

5

Wait for the raffle to end

Once all tickets for an item have been sold, the platform will automatically conduct a raffle and select a winner. The more tickets you have for an item, the higher your chances of winning!

6

Exchange items and tokens

If you win an item, contact the seller to arrange for delivery or pickup. If you're a seller, you'll receive the tokens from the winning buyer, which you can use to buy tickets for other items or cash out.
